  • Alpine Skiing
  • Alpine skiing, or downhill skiing, is the pastime of sliding down snow-covered slopes on skis with fixed-heel bindings.
  • Biathlon
  • The biathlon is a sport that combines cross-country skiing and rifle shooting.
  • Bobsleigh
  • Bobsleigh is a team sport that involves making timed runs down narrow, twisting, banked, iced tracks in a gravity-powered sleigh.
  • Cross Country Skiing
  • Cross-country skiing is a form of skiing where skiers rely on their own locomotion to move across snow-covered terrain.
  • Figure Skating
  • Figure skating is a sport in which individuals, pairs, or groups perform on figure skates on ice.
  • Ice Hockey
  • Ice hockey is a team sport played on ice skates, usually on an ice skating rink with lines and markings specific to the sport.
  • Luge
  • Luge is the fastest sport in the Winter Olympics, where athletes ride a flat sled through a course face up and feet first.
  • Nordic Combined
  • Nordic combined is a sport in which athletes compete in cross-country skiing and ski jumping.
  • Ski Jumping
  • Ski jumping is a sport in which competitors aim to achieve the farthest jump after sliding down on their skis from a specially designed curved ramp.
  • Speed Skating
  • Speed skating is a competitive form of ice skating in which the competitors race each other in travelling a certain distance on skates.
