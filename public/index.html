Québec City Winter Olympics 2030

The Winter Olympic Games were made for Québec City’s sprawling atmosphere and unique, complex culture. Québec City’s landmarks such as the Chateau de Frontenac, the Plains of Abraham and many others make the city striking and indistinguishable from any other. The old city of Québec has a multitude of restaurant choices with many different cuisines and tastes. A variety of shops and markets are available all throughout the year, including the Carnaval de Québec in the heart of Québec City.

The logo was made with keeping Québec City's image and culture in mind. The first choice and the best choice, the Chateau de Frontenac's shape is featured front and center on the Québec City Winter Olympic Games 2030 logo, overlayed by traditional Olympic colours were chosen to keep the integrity and beauty of the games intact and as amazing as spectators around the world see it today, watching their home country's world-class atheletes traverse their way to the world sports podium.

The year at which the games are taking place, "2030", are prominently shown in the logo as a window to the future.
